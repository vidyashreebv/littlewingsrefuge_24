Little Wings Refuge


Our Purpose

At Little Wings Refuge, our mission is to provide a safe haven for orphaned and vulnerable children, where they can find love, care, and support in a nurturing environment. We believe that every child deserves a chance to thrive and reach their full potential, regardless of their circumstances. Through our dedicated staff, comprehensive programs, and community partnerships, we offer holistic care that addresses the physical, emotional, and educational needs of each child under our care. From ensuring access to quality education and healthcare to facilitating loving adoptions, we are committed to empowering children to build brighter futures. Our goal is not only to provide temporary shelter but to create lasting positive change in the lives of every child who walks through our doors. Join us in making a difference and giving these children the wings they need to soar.
