Our Story

Little Wings Refuge began its journey in year 2023 with a simple goal: provide a safe haven for orphaned and vulnerable children. Since then, we've grown into a vibrant community of staff members and children, all working together to create a nurturing environment where every child can thrive.

We believe that every child deserves a chance to reach their full potential, regardless of their circumstances. Through our dedicated staff, comprehensive programs, and community partnerships, we offer holistic care that addresses the physical, emotional, and educational needs of each child under our care.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every child with dignity and respect, recognizing their unique needs and experiences.
  • Empowerment: We equip children with the skills and resources they need to build independent and fulfilling lives.
  • Family: We cultivate a sense of belonging and community within our refuge, fostering positive relationships and emotional well-being.
  • Hope: We believe in the potential of every child, and we strive to instill hope for a brighter future.

How You Can Help

Your support is vital in helping us continue our mission. You can make a difference by:

  • Making a donation
  • Volunteering your time and skills
  • Sponsoring a child
  • Raising awareness about our work

Together, we can create a world where every child has the opportunity to thrive.
